Time Series Foundation Model

We empower organizations to harness the full potential of their data through our cutting-edge multi-modality time series solution. Our platform stands at the forefront of analytics, seamlessly blending diverse data streams—from temporal patterns to spatial imagery and beyond—into a cohesive analytical framework.

General Time Series Tasks

Forecasting Suite

Includes tools for short-term and long-term forecasting, as well as probabilistic forecasting to account for uncertainty.​

Causal Analysis Engine

Helps to uncover causal relationships within time series data, which is crucial for understanding why certain events occur.

Counterfactual Framework

Offers a sophisticated counterfactual prediction feature, enabling users to explore 'what-if' scenarios.

Anomaly Detection System

Uses advanced algorithms to detect anomalies in real-time, helping to prevent fraud and identify system failures.

Classification Toolkit

Allows for the categorization of time series data into different classes for easier analysis and interpretation.
​

Synthetic Data Generation

Generates high-quality synthetic time series data for testing, training, and simulation purposes.

Foundation of time series​

Our platform excels in spatio-temporal analytics, which is the fusion of spatial data (location, geography) with temporal data (time series).​
Our advanced spatio-temporal models are the backbone for predictive analytics, allowing users to forecast future events with a high degree of precision and to make more informed decisions based on the 'where' and 'when' of their data.

Spatio-Temporal​

Embrace the power of multi modality with our platform, where diverse data streams converge to tell a complete story.​
We integrate time series data with text, images, audio, and video, providing a holistic view of complex phenomena.
By leveraging cross-modal correlations, our clients gain a competitive edge through comprehensive understanding and actionable intelligence.​

Multi Modality​

Our platform prioritizes interpretability, offering comprehensible analytics. It reveals data interactions over time, explaining how variables influence each other, and enabling users to understand the 'why' behind the 'what'.
Our time series models provide clear explanations for their outputs, enhancing trust and facilitating decision-making in critical sectors like finance and healthcare.

Relationships​

Experience unmatched clarity and adaptability with our multi-resolution time series analysis. Our platform reveals different insights at varying scales, from long-term trends to high-frequency nuances.
Our customized analytical lens is fine-tuned to the specific temporal needs of each investigation. This flexibility allows clients to zoom in anomalies or zoom out to identify overarching patterns.
