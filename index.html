TimeAGI

TimeAGI is a versatile framework and toolkit for time series forecasting and analysis, designed to meet the demands of complex, multi-faceted temporal tasks. Equipped with powerful capabilities for accurate time series prediction, anomaly detection, and sophisticated composite reasoning, TimeAGI pushes the boundaries of what’s possible with foundation models in time series analysis. It offers a suite of adaptable, foundation model-based solutions tailored for diverse applications, from financial forecasting and energy management to climate science, natural sciences, and social sciences. With TimeAGI, organizations in critical sectors gain access to reliable, next-generation time series insights, enabling impactful data-driven decisions.

TimeAGI Models

TEMPO

TEMPO is a framework using prompt-based transformers for time series forecasting across domains like healthcare and finance. With decomposition techniques and multimodal data, it excels in zero-shot forecasting and helps identify key drivers behind trends, supporting data-driven decisions.

TimeDiT

TimeDiT is a foundation model for time series analysis, leveraging a diffusion-based approach with Transformer architecture to handle complex tasks like forecasting, imputation, and anomaly detection. By using denoising diffusion, TimeDiT effectively manages missing values and temporal dependencies.

Paper Link ➔

TS-Reasoner

TS-Reasoner is an advanced framework for complex time series reasoning, integrating large language models (LLMs) to break down multi-step tasks like decision-making and causal analysis into structured programs. TS-Reasoner synthesizes time series data with domain expertise, enabling custom modules and flexible task decomposition.
